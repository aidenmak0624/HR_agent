PAID TIME OFF, PARENTAL LEAVE, AND OTHER LEAVE BENEFITS
TechNova Inc. | Effective January 1, 2026
===========================================================

SECTION 1: PAID TIME OFF (PTO)

VACATION DAYS
TechNova provides paid vacation based on role level and tenure:

Entry Level / Individual Contributors (0-2 years):
- 15 vacation days per year (accrual: 1.25 days/month)

Mid-Level / Individual Contributors (2-5 years):
- 20 vacation days per year (accrual: 1.67 days/month)

Senior / Managers (5+ years or manager level):
- 25 vacation days per year (accrual: 2.08 days/month)

Directors and Above:
- 25 vacation days per year (no accrual cap)

Tenure Bonus: +1 additional vacation day per 2 years of service (maximum +5 days)

VACATION ACCRUAL RULES
- Accrual begins on the first day of employment
- Maximum carryover: 5 unused days may be carried into the next calendar year
- Unused days beyond the carryover limit are forfeited on December 31
- Upon separation, accrued but unused vacation is paid out at the employee's current rate
- Vacation may be taken in half-day or full-day increments
- Requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance for 3+ consecutive days
- Manager approval required; business needs may require schedule adjustment

SICK LEAVE
- All employees receive 10 paid sick days per year (available on Day 1 of employment)
- Sick leave may be used for:
  - Personal illness or injury
  - Medical, dental, or vision appointments
  - Care of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent) who is ill
  - Mental health days
- Medical certificate required for absences of 3 or more consecutive days
- Unused sick days do not carry over and are not paid out upon separation

PERSONAL DAYS
- Entry Level: 3 personal days per year
- Manager Level: 5 personal days per year
- Director and Above: 5 personal days per year
- Personal days may be used for any reason (moving, personal business, religious observance, etc.)
- 2 weeks advance notice preferred; same-day use allowed for emergencies
- Personal days do not carry over and are not paid out upon separation

COMPANY HOLIDAYS (2026)
TechNova observes the following 10 paid holidays:
1. New Year's Day - January 1
2. Martin Luther King Jr. Day - January 19
3. Presidents' Day - February 16
4. Memorial Day - May 25
5. Independence Day - July 4 (observed July 3 if Saturday)
6. Labor Day - September 7
7. Thanksgiving Day - November 26
8. Day after Thanksgiving - November 27
9. Christmas Eve - December 24
10. Christmas Day - December 25

Floating Holidays: Each employee receives 2 floating holidays per year, usable at their discretion with manager approval. Must be used within the calendar year.

Winter Office Closure: TechNova typically closes December 26 through January 1 (varies by year). Employees are given this time off with pay in addition to regular PTO.

SECTION 2: PARENTAL LEAVE

BIRTH PARENT LEAVE
- 16 weeks fully paid leave (inclusive of any short-term disability period)
- May be taken starting up to 4 weeks before the expected due date
- Additional 4 weeks of gradual return (reduced schedule at full pay) available upon request
- Job protection: Same or equivalent position upon return

NON-BIRTH PARENT LEAVE
- 8 weeks fully paid leave
- Must be taken within 12 months of the birth or placement of the child
- May be taken continuously or in 1-week increments with manager approval

ADOPTION AND FOSTER CARE LEAVE
- Primary caregiver: 16 weeks fully paid
- Secondary caregiver: 8 weeks fully paid
- Adoption assistance: TechNova reimburses up to $10,000 in qualified adoption expenses

ELIGIBILITY
- Must have been employed at TechNova for at least 6 months
- Available to all full-time employees regardless of gender or marital status

SECTION 3: OTHER LEAVE TYPES

BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
- Immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 paid days
- Extended family (grandparent, in-law, aunt, uncle): 3 paid days
- Close friend or colleague: 1 paid day
- Additional unpaid leave available upon request

JURY DUTY
- TechNova pays full salary for the duration of jury service
- Employees must provide the jury duty summons to HR
- Jury duty fees received from the court may be retained by the employee

VOTING LEAVE
- Up to 2 hours of paid time off for voting on election days
- Employees should arrange with their manager to minimize business disruption

MILITARY LEAVE
- TechNova complies with USERRA (Uniformed Services Employment and Reemployment Rights Act)
- Up to 5 years of cumulative leave for military service with job protection
- TechNova pays the difference between military pay and regular salary for up to 12 months of active duty

SABBATICAL
- After 7 years of continuous employment, employees are eligible for a 4-week paid sabbatical
- Must be taken as a single continuous block
- Available once every 7 years

TechNova Inc. | Effective January 1, 2026

SECTION 1: LIFE INSURANCE AND DISABILITY

BASIC LIFE INSURANCE (Company-Paid)
- Coverage: 2x annual base salary (up to $500,000 maximum)
- No cost to employees - fully paid by TechNova
- Accidental Death and Dismemberment (AD&D): Equal to basic life amount
- Beneficiary designation: Update via HR portal or contact benefits@technova.com

SUPPLEMENTAL LIFE INSURANCE (Employee-Paid)
- Optional additional coverage: 1x to 5x annual salary
- Guaranteed issue: Up to 3x salary without medical underwriting (new hires within 30 days)
- Spousal coverage: Up to $100,000 (guaranteed issue up to $25,000)
- Child coverage: $10,000 per child

SHORT-TERM DISABILITY (STD)
- Coverage: 60% of base salary
- Waiting period: 7 calendar days
- Benefit duration: Up to 26 weeks
- Fully paid by TechNova

LONG-TERM DISABILITY (LTD)
- Coverage: 60% of base salary (up to $15,000/month)
- Waiting period: Begins after STD exhausted (26 weeks)
- Benefit duration: Until age 65 or Social Security Normal Retirement Age
- Fully paid by TechNova

SECTION 2: FLEXIBLE SPENDING ACCOUNTS

HEALTH CARE FSA
- Annual contribution limit: $3,400 (2026)
- Use for: Medical copays, prescriptions, dental work, vision expenses, over-the-counter medications
- Use-it-or-lose-it: Unused funds forfeited at end of plan year (March 15 grace period)
- Cannot be combined with HSA (HDHP participants use HSA instead)

DEPENDENT CARE FSA
- Annual contribution limit: $7,500 (2026 - historic first increase from $5,000)
- Use for: Daycare, preschool, after-school care, elder care, summer day camps
- Eligibility: Must have a dependent under 13 or a disabled dependent
- Tax savings: Reduces taxable income by contribution amount

SECTION 3: EMPLOYEE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (EAP)

OVERVIEW
TechNova provides a confidential Employee Assistance Program through ComPsych at no cost to employees and their household members.

SERVICES INCLUDED
- Mental health counseling: 8 free sessions per issue per year
- Financial counseling: Budgeting, debt management, retirement planning
- Legal consultation: 30-minute consultation per issue, 25% discount on retained services
- Work-life balance resources: Child care referrals, elder care assistance, moving/relocation help
- Crisis support: 24/7 crisis hotline
- Substance abuse assessment and referral
- Identity theft recovery assistance

HOW TO ACCESS
- Phone: 1-800-555-0199 (available 24/7, 365 days)
- Online: www.guidanceresources.com (company code: TECHNOVA)
- In-person sessions available through network providers
- All services are 100% confidential - TechNova never receives individual usage data

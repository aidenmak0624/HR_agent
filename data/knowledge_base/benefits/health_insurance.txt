HEALTH INSURANCE BENEFITS - MEDICAL, DENTAL, AND VISION
TechNova Inc. | Plan Year 2026
===========================================================

OVERVIEW
TechNova offers comprehensive health insurance to all full-time employees (30+ hours/week) and their eligible dependents. Coverage begins on the first day of the month following your start date. TechNova covers a significant portion of premium costs.

MEDICAL PLANS

Plan 1: PPO (Preferred Provider Organization) - Most Popular
- Monthly Premium (Employee Only): $180/month (TechNova pays 82%)
- Monthly Premium (Employee + Family): $520/month (TechNova pays 76%)
- Annual Deductible: $500 individual / $1,000 family
- Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $4,000 individual / $8,000 family
- Primary Care Visit: $25 copay
- Specialist Visit: $50 copay
- Urgent Care: $75 copay
- Emergency Room: $250 copay (waived if admitted)
- Prescription Drugs: $10 generic / $35 preferred brand / $60 non-preferred / $100 specialty
- Preventive Care: 100% covered (no copay)
- In-Network Coverage: 80% after deductible
- Out-of-Network Coverage: 60% after deductible
- Network: Blue Cross Blue Shield national PPO network

Plan 2: HMO (Health Maintenance Organization) - Lower Cost
- Monthly Premium (Employee Only): $120/month (TechNova pays 85%)
- Monthly Premium (Employee + Family): $380/month (TechNova pays 80%)
- Annual Deductible: $250 individual / $500 family
- Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $3,000 individual / $6,000 family
- Primary Care Visit: $20 copay
- Specialist Visit: $40 copay (referral required)
- Emergency Room: $200 copay
- Prescription Drugs: $10 generic / $30 preferred brand / $50 non-preferred
- Preventive Care: 100% covered
- Requires primary care physician (PCP) selection and referrals for specialists
- Network: Kaiser Permanente (where available) or local HMO network

Plan 3: High Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) with HSA - Tax Advantaged
- Monthly Premium (Employee Only): $75/month (TechNova pays 90%)
- Monthly Premium (Employee + Family): $240/month (TechNova pays 85%)
- Annual Deductible: $1,600 individual / $3,200 family
- Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $7,000 individual / $14,000 family
- All services (except preventive): Subject to deductible, then 80% coinsurance
- Preventive Care: 100% covered (no deductible)
- HSA Contribution: TechNova contributes $750/year for individual, $1,500/year for family
- Employee HSA Contribution Limit (2026): $4,300 individual / $8,550 family
- HSA funds roll over year to year and are portable

DENTAL PLAN
- Monthly Premium (Employee Only): $25/month (TechNova pays 100%)
- Monthly Premium (Employee + Family): $65/month (TechNova pays 75%)
- Annual Maximum Benefit: $2,000 per person
- Preventive Care (cleanings, exams, X-rays): 100% covered, 2 visits per year
- Basic Procedures (fillings, extractions): 80% covered after $50 deductible
- Major Procedures (crowns, bridges, root canals): 50% covered after $50 deductible
- Orthodontia (children under 19): 50% covered, $2,000 lifetime maximum
- Network: Delta Dental PPO

VISION PLAN
- Monthly Premium (Employee Only): $10/month (TechNova pays 100%)
- Monthly Premium (Employee + Family): $25/month (TechNova pays 80%)
- Annual Eye Exam: $10 copay (one per year)
- Eyeglass Frames: $175 allowance every 24 months
- Eyeglass Lenses: Covered in full (standard single vision, bifocal, or progressive)
- Contact Lenses: $175 allowance per year (in lieu of glasses)
- Laser Eye Surgery (LASIK): 15% discount through VSP network providers
- Network: VSP Vision Care

ENROLLMENT
- New Hires: Enroll within 30 days of start date; coverage effective first of the following month
- Open Enrollment: November 1-15 annually (coverage effective January 1)
- Qualifying Life Events: Marriage, divorce, birth/adoption, loss of other coverage, and other qualifying events allow mid-year enrollment changes within 30 days of the event

DEPENDENT ELIGIBILITY
- Legal spouse or registered domestic partner
- Children under age 26 (biological, adopted, stepchildren, foster children)
- Children with disabilities (no age limit if disabled before age 26)

WELLNESS INCENTIVES
Employees who complete the annual health assessment receive a $25/month premium discount on any medical plan.

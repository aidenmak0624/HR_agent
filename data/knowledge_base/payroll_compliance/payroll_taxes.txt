PAYROLL, TAX WITHHOLDING, AND COMPLIANCE GUIDE
TechNova Inc. | Effective January 1, 2026
===========================================================

SECTION 1: PAY SCHEDULE AND DIRECT DEPOSIT

PAY FREQUENCY
- Salaried employees: Semi-monthly (24 pay periods per year)
  - Pay dates: 15th and last business day of each month
  - If pay date falls on a weekend or holiday, payment is issued on the preceding business day
- Hourly/non-exempt employees: Bi-weekly (26 pay periods per year)
  - Pay dates: Every other Friday

DIRECT DEPOSIT
- TechNova strongly encourages direct deposit for all employees
- Setup: Complete the Direct Deposit Authorization Form (available on the HR portal)
- Changes: Allow 1-2 pay cycles for new direct deposit setup or changes to take effect
- Multiple accounts: You may split deposits across up to 3 bank accounts
- Paper checks: Available upon request but may take 1-2 additional business days

PAY STUBS
- Available electronically via the payroll portal (ADP Workforce Now)
- Accessible within 2 days of each pay date
- Shows: Gross pay, deductions (taxes, benefits, 401k), net pay, YTD totals
- Paper stubs mailed upon request

SECTION 2: TAX WITHHOLDING

FORM W-4 (FEDERAL)
- Must be completed by all employees at hire
- Determines federal income tax withholding from each paycheck
- Update anytime your personal/financial situation changes (marriage, divorce, new dependents, second job)
- If claiming exempt status, a new W-4 must be submitted by February 15 each year
- TechNova cannot advise on how to complete your W-4; consult a tax professional or use the IRS Tax Withholding Estimator at www.irs.gov/individuals/tax-withholding-estimator

STATE TAX WITHHOLDING
- Employees working in states with income tax must complete the applicable state W-4 form
- States with no income tax: Alaska, Florida, Nevada, New Hampshire, South Dakota, Tennessee, Texas, Washington, Wyoming
- Remote employees: Withholding is based on the state where work is performed
- Multi-state employees: May require withholding in multiple states

FORM W-2
- TechNova issues W-2 forms by January 31 each year for the prior tax year
- Available electronically on the payroll portal and mailed to your address on file
- If you do not receive your W-2 by February 15, contact payroll@technova.com
- W-2 shows: Total wages, federal/state/local taxes withheld, Social Security and Medicare wages, pre-tax deductions

FICA TAXES (NON-NEGOTIABLE DEDUCTIONS)
- Social Security tax: 6.2% on earnings up to $168,600 (2026 wage base - subject to annual adjustment)
- Medicare tax: 1.45% on all earnings (no wage cap)
- Additional Medicare tax: 0.9% on earnings over $200,000 (employee only; no employer match)
- TechNova matches the employer portion: 6.2% Social Security + 1.45% Medicare

SECTION 3: WORKERS' COMPENSATION

COVERAGE
- TechNova carries workers' compensation insurance as required by law
- Covers all employees from the first day of employment
- Coverage includes: Medical bills, lost wages (typically 66.7% of average weekly wage), rehabilitation, death benefits

WHAT TO DO IF INJURED AT WORK
1. Seek immediate medical attention if needed (call 911 for emergencies)
2. Report the injury to your manager within 24 hours
3. Complete an Incident Report Form (available on the HR portal or from your manager)
4. HR files the workers' compensation claim with our insurance carrier
5. Follow your doctor's treatment plan and provide any work restriction documentation to HR
6. Return-to-work clearance required from your healthcare provider before resuming full duties

REMOTE WORKER INJURIES
- Work-related injuries that occur in your home office during work hours may be covered
- You must be able to demonstrate the injury occurred during the course of performing work duties
- Report any injury to your manager and HR immediately

SECTION 4: EMPLOYMENT VERIFICATION AND RECORDS

FORM I-9 (EMPLOYMENT ELIGIBILITY VERIFICATION)
- Required for ALL new employees regardless of citizenship
- Section 1: Completed by employee no later than the first day of work
- Section 2: Completed by TechNova HR within 3 business days of start date
- Acceptable documents: US Passport (List A), OR Driver's License (List B) + Social Security Card (List C), OR other combinations per the I-9 instructions
- TechNova uses E-Verify to confirm employment eligibility electronically
- I-9 records retained for 3 years after hire date or 1 year after termination, whichever is later

NEW HIRE REPORTING
- TechNova reports all new and rehired employees to the state within 20 days as required by federal law
- Information reported: Employee name, address, SSN, hire date; Employer name and FEIN
- Used by the state for child support enforcement

BACKGROUND CHECKS
- TechNova conducts background checks for all new hires after a conditional offer of employment
- Checks may include: Criminal history, education verification, employment history, professional license verification
- Process follows FCRA (Fair Credit Reporting Act) requirements:
  - Written consent obtained before ordering the check
  - Pre-adverse action notice with copy of the report if potentially negative decision
  - 5 business days for the candidate to respond
  - Final adverse action notice if the decision stands
- Individualized assessment: Considers nature of offense, time elapsed, and relevance to the position
- Clean Slate Laws: TechNova complies with state laws regarding sealed/expunged records

EMPLOYEE RECORDS
- Personnel files maintained securely by HR
- Employees may request to review their own file with 24 hours notice
- Medical records kept in separate, confidential files
- Records retained per TechNova's retention schedule and applicable law (minimum 3 years post-separation)

SECTION 5: EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT

PROCESS
1. Submit expenses via the expense portal within 30 days of incurring the expense
2. Attach receipts for all expenses over $25
3. Manager approval required for all submissions
4. Reimbursement processed within 2 pay cycles of approval

COMMON REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
- Business travel (flights, hotels, ground transportation, meals)
- Client entertainment (with prior approval)
- Professional development materials
- Home office supplies (per Remote Work Policy)
- Mileage: Reimbursed at the IRS standard rate ($0.70/mile for 2026)

CORPORATE CREDIT CARD
- Available for managers and above, and employees with frequent travel
- Must be used for business expenses only
- Reconciled monthly with receipts

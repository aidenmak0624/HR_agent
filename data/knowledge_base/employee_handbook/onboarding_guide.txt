NEW EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE
TechNova Inc. | Effective January 1, 2026
===========================================================

WELCOME TO TECHNOVA!
Congratulations on joining TechNova Inc.! This guide covers everything you need to know for a smooth start.

PRE-START CHECKLIST (Complete Before Day 1)
- Accept your offer letter and return signed copy to HR
- Complete Form I-9 Section 1 (bring valid ID documents on Day 1)
- Complete Form W-4 (federal tax withholding)
- Complete state tax withholding form (if applicable)
- Complete direct deposit authorization
- Complete benefits enrollment (you have 30 days from start date)
- Complete background check authorization
- Review and sign: Employment Agreement, NDA, Invention Assignment Agreement, Code of Conduct
- Upload your professional headshot for the company directory

DAY 1 SCHEDULE
8:30 AM - Arrive at the office (or log into virtual onboarding session)
9:00 AM - Welcome from your hiring manager
9:30 AM - HR orientation session
  - Company overview, mission, and values
  - Benefits enrollment walkthrough
  - I-9 document verification
  - Badge/access card issuance (in-office employees)
  - IT equipment setup and account provisioning
10:30 AM - IT setup and systems access
  - Laptop configuration
  - Email setup (Google Workspace)
  - Slack workspace access
  - VPN and security tool installation
  - HR portal and payroll system access
12:00 PM - Team lunch (company-sponsored)
1:00 PM - Meet your onboarding buddy
1:30 PM - Department orientation with your manager
  - Team introductions
  - Role expectations and 30/60/90 day goals
  - Regular meeting schedule
  - Key stakeholders and cross-functional partners
3:00 PM - Self-paced: Complete required online training modules
4:30 PM - Day 1 check-in with your manager

WEEK 1 MILESTONES
- Complete all compliance training (anti-harassment, data security, code of conduct)
- Set up 1-on-1 cadence with your manager
- Meet key stakeholders in your department
- Begin your 30-day learning plan
- Access and explore the HR portal, benefits enrollment, and company intranet

FIRST 30 DAYS
- Complete all required training modules (see Training Requirements below)
- Set your 3-5 OKRs with your manager for the first review period
- Shadow 2-3 colleagues in related roles
- Attend your first company all-hands meeting (first Monday of each month)
- Enroll in benefits (deadline: 30 days from start date)
- Complete 30-day check-in with your manager

FIRST 90 DAYS
- Deliver on your first key project or milestone
- Complete your first performance check-in with your manager
- Join at least one Employee Resource Group (ERG) or committee
- Provide feedback on your onboarding experience via the survey
- Employer match for 401(k) begins (if auto-enrolled at hire)

REQUIRED TRAINING (COMPLETE WITHIN 30 DAYS)
1. Anti-Harassment and Anti-Discrimination Training (90 minutes)
2. Data Security and Privacy Training (60 minutes)
3. Code of Conduct Acknowledgment (30 minutes)
4. Emergency Procedures and Workplace Safety (30 minutes)
5. Role-specific compliance training (varies by department)

SYSTEMS AND TOOLS
- Email: Google Workspace (Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Meet)
- Chat: Slack (join #general, #your-department, #random, #announcements)
- HR Portal: BambooHR (PTO requests, personal info, documents)
- Payroll: ADP Workforce Now (pay stubs, tax forms, direct deposit)
- Project Management: Jira (Engineering), Asana (Business teams)
- Code Repository: GitHub (Engineering)
- Knowledge Base: Confluence / Notion
- Video Conferencing: Google Meet, Zoom
- Password Manager: 1Password (mandatory for all employees)
- VPN: Required for remote access to internal systems

ONBOARDING BUDDY PROGRAM
Every new employee is assigned an onboarding buddy from their department:
- Your buddy will reach out before your first day
- They are your go-to person for informal questions
- Weekly coffee chat for the first month
- Buddy assignment lasts 90 days

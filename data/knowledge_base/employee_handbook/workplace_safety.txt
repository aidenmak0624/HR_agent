WORKPLACE SAFETY AND DATA PRIVACY POLICY
TechNova Inc. | Effective January 1, 2026
===========================================================

SECTION 1: WORKPLACE SAFETY

COMMITMENT TO SAFETY
TechNova is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all employees, contractors, and visitors. We comply with all OSHA (Occupational Safety and Health Administration) requirements and applicable state safety regulations.

OFFICE SAFETY
- Ergonomic workstations: Standing desks, adjustable monitors, ergonomic keyboards and mice available upon request
- Report any unsafe conditions (wet floors, damaged furniture, exposed wiring) to Facilities immediately
- Keep aisles, exits, and emergency equipment access clear at all times
- No blocking of fire exits, sprinkler heads, or fire extinguishers
- Report any equipment malfunctions to IT or Facilities

ERGONOMIC ASSESSMENT
- All employees may request a free ergonomic workstation assessment
- Remote employees: Receive a virtual ergonomic consultation
- Common adjustments: Monitor height, chair positioning, keyboard/mouse placement, lighting
- Request through: facilities@technova.com or the HR portal

EMERGENCY PROCEDURES
Fire:
1. If you discover a fire, activate the nearest fire alarm pull station
2. Call 911
3. Evacuate using the nearest exit (do not use elevators)
4. Proceed to the designated assembly area (front parking lot, Section A)
5. Report to your floor warden for headcount

Medical Emergency:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Notify the front desk (ext. 0) or nearest colleague
3. If trained in first aid/CPR, assist the person until EMS arrives
4. AED devices located: Main lobby, 2nd floor break room, gym

Severe Weather:
1. Move to interior rooms away from windows
2. Follow instructions from floor wardens
3. Do not leave the building until the all-clear is given

Active Threat:
1. Run: Evacuate if a safe path exists
2. Hide: If you cannot evacuate, hide in a secure location, lock/barricade the door
3. Fight: As a last resort only

INCIDENT REPORTING
- All workplace injuries, near-misses, and safety concerns must be reported within 24 hours
- Report to: Your manager AND hr@technova.com
- Complete an Incident Report Form on the HR portal
- TechNova will investigate and take corrective action
- No retaliation for reporting safety concerns

REMOTE WORKER SAFETY
- Maintain a dedicated workspace free from hazards
- Ensure proper electrical safety (no overloaded outlets, damaged cords)
- Report any work-related injury occurring during work hours
- TechNova provides a $1,000 home office setup stipend for ergonomic equipment

SECTION 2: DATA PRIVACY AND INFORMATION SECURITY

DATA CLASSIFICATION
TechNova classifies all data into four categories:
1. Public: Information intended for public release (marketing materials, press releases)
2. Internal: General company information (org charts, internal memos, meeting notes)
3. Confidential: Sensitive business information (financial data, client contracts, product roadmaps)
4. Restricted: Highly sensitive data (PII, health records, trade secrets, credentials)

EMPLOYEE DATA PRIVACY
TechNova collects and maintains employee personal information necessary for employment:
- Personal details: Name, address, SSN, date of birth, emergency contacts
- Employment data: Job title, salary, performance reviews, disciplinary records
- Benefits data: Health plan enrollment, beneficiary designations, 401k contributions
- Medical records: Maintained in separate confidential files, accessible only by HR

Your Rights:
- Access: You may review your personnel file with 24 hours notice to HR
- Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
- Confidentiality: Your personal data is shared only on a need-to-know basis
- California residents (CCPA): Additional rights to know, delete, and opt-out; contact privacy@technova.com

INFORMATION SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
All Employees Must:
- Use strong, unique passwords (minimum 12 characters with complexity) via 1Password
- Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) on all company accounts
- Lock your computer when leaving your desk (Ctrl+L or Cmd+L)
- Connect via VPN when accessing company resources remotely
- Not share credentials with anyone, including IT staff
- Not use personal devices for company work without MDM enrollment
- Not store company data on personal cloud storage (Google Drive personal, Dropbox, etc.)
- Report any suspected security incident immediately to security@technova.com

ACCEPTABLE USE OF TECHNOLOGY
- Company devices and networks are for business use; limited personal use is permitted
- TechNova reserves the right to monitor company devices, networks, and email
- Prohibited activities: Accessing illegal content, installing unauthorized software, crypto mining, circumventing security controls
- Personal devices on company WiFi: Must use the guest network

DATA BREACH RESPONSE
If you suspect a data breach:
1. Do not attempt to investigate or contain the breach yourself
2. Report immediately to security@technova.com or call ext. 3100
3. Preserve any evidence (do not delete emails, logs, or files)
4. TechNova's Incident Response Team will investigate and take appropriate action
5. Affected individuals will be notified per legal requirements (typically within 72 hours)

RECORD RETENTION
- Active employee records: Maintained throughout employment
- Post-separation retention: Minimum 3 years (7 years for payroll/tax records)
- Medical records: Minimum 6 years post-separation
- I-9 records: 3 years from hire date or 1 year from termination (whichever is later)
- Destruction: Secure shredding for paper; secure deletion for electronic records

OSHA PENALTY INFORMATION
For reference, current OSHA penalty amounts (as of January 2025):
- Serious/Other-Than-Serious Violations: Up to $16,550 per violation
- Willful or Repeated Violations: Up to $165,514 per violation
- Failure to Abate: Up to $16,550 per day beyond the abatement date

TECHNOVA INC. BENEFITS SUMMARY GUIDE 2026

HEALTH INSURANCE
TechNova offers three medical plan options for full-time employees (30+ hours/week):

PPO Plan: $180/month employee-only ($520 family). $500/$1,000 deductible. $25 PCP copay, $50 specialist. 80/60 in/out-of-network. Blue Cross Blue Shield network.

HMO Plan: $120/month employee-only ($380 family). $250/$500 deductible. $20 PCP copay. Requires PCP selection and referrals. Kaiser Permanente network.

HDHP with HSA: $75/month employee-only ($240 family). $1,600/$3,200 deductible. TechNova contributes $750/$1,500 to your HSA. HSA funds roll over and are portable.

All plans: Preventive care 100% covered. Coverage effective first of month following start date.

Dental: $25/month employee-only (TechNova pays 100%). Preventive 100%, basic 80%, major 50%. $2,000 annual max. Delta Dental PPO.

Vision: $10/month employee-only (TechNova pays 100%). $10 exam copay. $175 frame allowance every 24 months. VSP network.

RETIREMENT
401(k) through Fidelity. Dollar-for-dollar match on first 4%, plus 50 cents on the dollar for next 2% (max 5% employer match). Auto-enrolled at 6%. 3-year graded vesting on employer match. 2026 contribution limit: $24,500 ($32,000 age 50+). Roth option available.

ESPP: 15% discount. Up to 15% of base salary. Semi-annual purchase periods.

LIFE AND DISABILITY
Life Insurance: 2x annual salary (company-paid, up to $500,000). AD&D included. Supplemental available up to 5x salary.

Short-Term Disability: 60% of salary, 7-day waiting period, up to 26 weeks. Company-paid.

Long-Term Disability: 60% of salary (up to $15,000/month), begins after STD. Company-paid.

PAID TIME OFF
Vacation: 15 days (0-2 years), 20 days (2-5 years), 25 days (5+ years or manager level). +1 day per 2 years tenure (max +5).
Sick Leave: 10 days/year.
Personal Days: 3 days (employee), 5 days (manager+).
Holidays: 10 company holidays + 2 floating holidays + winter office closure.
Parental Leave: 16 weeks birth parent, 8 weeks non-birth parent (fully paid after 6 months tenure).

ADDITIONAL BENEFITS
EAP: 8 free counseling sessions per issue. Financial, legal, and work-life services. 24/7 at 1-800-555-0199.
Tuition Reimbursement: $7,500/year with manager approval.
Gym Subsidy: $75/month reimbursement.
Wellness Stipend: $500/year for health-related purchases.
Home Office: $1,000 setup + $50/month ongoing + $75/month internet.
Commuter: $340/month pre-tax transit and parking.
Sabbatical: 4 weeks paid after 7 years.

ENROLLMENT
New hires: Within 30 days of start date. Open enrollment: November 1-15 annually. Life events: Within 30 days of qualifying event.

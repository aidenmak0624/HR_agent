TECHNOVA INC. PAID TIME OFF (PTO) DETAILED POLICY 2026

VACATION ACCRUAL BY LEVEL
Entry Level / IC (0-2 years): 15 days/year, accrues at 1.25 days/month.
Mid-Level / IC (2-5 years): 20 days/year, accrues at 1.67 days/month.
Senior / Manager (5+ years or manager level): 25 days/year, accrues at 2.08 days/month.
Directors and Above: 25 days/year, no accrual cap.
Tenure Bonus: +1 vacation day per 2 years of service (maximum +5 additional days).

ACCRUAL AND CARRYOVER
Accrual begins on first day of employment. PTO must be used in the year earned. Maximum carryover: 5 unused vacation days into the next calendar year. Days beyond the carryover cap are forfeited on December 31. Exception: If business needs prevented an employee from using PTO, a one-time carryover extension may be granted with VP approval.

SICK LEAVE
10 paid sick days per year, available from Day 1 of employment. May be used for personal illness, medical appointments, care of ill family members, or mental health days. Medical certificate required for absences of 3 or more consecutive days. Sick days do not accrue, carry over, or pay out upon separation.

PERSONAL DAYS
Entry Level: 3 personal days per year. Manager and above: 5 personal days per year. May be used for any reason. 2 weeks advance notice preferred; same-day use for emergencies. Do not carry over or pay out.

REQUESTING TIME OFF
Submit PTO requests through the HR portal (BambooHR). Requests for 1-2 days: Submit at least 3 business days in advance. Requests for 3+ consecutive days: Submit at least 2 weeks in advance. Requests for 5+ consecutive days: Submit at least 4 weeks in advance. Manager must approve or deny within 2 business days. During high-volume periods (e.g., end of quarter), approval is based on business needs and team coverage.

HOLIDAYS (2026)
10 paid company holidays: New Year's Day (Jan 1), MLK Jr. Day (Jan 19), Presidents' Day (Feb 16), Memorial Day (May 25), Independence Day (Jul 4), Labor Day (Sep 7), Thanksgiving (Nov 26), Day After Thanksgiving (Nov 27), Christmas Eve (Dec 24), Christmas Day (Dec 25).

2 floating holidays per employee per year (use with manager approval, cannot carry over).

Winter Office Closure: December 26 - January 1 (paid, in addition to regular PTO).

SEPARATION PAYOUT
Accrued, unused vacation days are paid out at the employee's current rate upon voluntary or involuntary separation. Sick and personal days are not paid out. Payout included in the final paycheck per state law.

PTO BLACKOUT DATES
No company-wide blackout dates. Individual teams may have blackout periods (e.g., end of quarter for Sales, release weeks for Engineering). Managers will communicate team-specific blackouts at least 30 days in advance.

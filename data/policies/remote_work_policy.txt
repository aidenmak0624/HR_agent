TECHNOVA INC. REMOTE WORK AND FLEXIBLE SCHEDULE POLICY 2026

HYBRID WORK MODEL
TechNova operates a hybrid work model. Standard schedule: 3 days in-office (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday), 2 days remote (Monday, Friday). Fully remote positions available for specific roles with VP approval.

ELIGIBILITY
All full-time employees after 6 months of employment with satisfactory performance. New employees work on-site for the first 6 months to build relationships and learn the culture. Exceptions may be granted by the VP of the employee's department.

CORE HOURS
All employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM local time. Flexible window for start/end: Begin between 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM, end between 4:00 PM and 7:00 PM. Full-time employees are expected to work 40 hours per week.

HOME OFFICE REQUIREMENTS
Reliable high-speed internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps download). Dedicated workspace (quiet, free from regular interruptions during work hours). Proper ergonomic setup (see Workplace Safety Policy for guidelines). Company-provided laptop must be used for all work. VPN required for accessing internal systems.

HOME OFFICE STIPEND
One-time setup allowance: $1,000 for ergonomic equipment (desk, chair, monitor, etc.). Monthly stipend: $50 for ongoing home office expenses. Internet reimbursement: Up to $75/month. Submit receipts via the expense portal.

COMMUNICATION EXPECTATIONS
Respond to Slack messages within 2 hours during core hours. Respond to emails within 24 hours on business days. Camera on for team meetings and client calls (recommended, not required for large all-hands). Update your Slack status to reflect your availability. Block focus time on your calendar when you need uninterrupted work.

EQUIPMENT AND SECURITY
TechNova provides: Laptop, monitor (upon request), keyboard, mouse, headset. All equipment must be returned upon separation. Use company VPN for all remote work. Lock your screen when away from your workstation. Do not use public WiFi without VPN. Do not allow non-employees to use company equipment.

INTERNATIONAL REMOTE WORK
Working from outside the United States requires prior approval from HR and Legal (minimum 30 days notice). Maximum 4 weeks per year in a foreign country. Tax, immigration, and data privacy implications will be assessed.

TERMINATION OF REMOTE WORK ARRANGEMENT
TechNova may require an employee to return to full-time in-office work with 2 weeks notice if: Performance does not meet expectations, Business needs require in-person presence, The employee violates the remote work agreement. The employee may also choose to return to full-time in-office at any time.
